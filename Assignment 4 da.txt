"Final Project Idea"

National Parks and types of customers who enter

This project will be a data analysis of the 5 chosen national parks: Zion's, Grand Canyon, Arches, Bryce Canyon, and Capitol Reef.
This project will analyze categories such as: Total entries per year, total entries per season, total entries per month,
 average numbers of adults vs children vs seniors.
Other categories will also include: Park size, average seasonal temperatures, and number of trails.
